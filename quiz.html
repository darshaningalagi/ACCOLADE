Quiz Rules - ACCOLADE #11.25

  • Each team must have exactly 4 members.
  • The quiz consists of multiple-choice questions.
  • Participants must collaborate within their team only.
  • Use of external resources or communication outside the team during the quiz is strictly prohibited.
  • Correct answers will be awarded points. Cheating or misbehavior will result in disqualification.
  • In case of a tie, a tiebreaker question or procedure will be used to determine the winner.
  • Participants are required to respect the quiz questions and content, which are the property of the organizers.
  • By participating, contestants agree to abide by these rules and regulations.
