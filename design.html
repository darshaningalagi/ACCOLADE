Design Event Rules - ACCOLADE #11.25

Round 1 (AKARAKALA)

  • Phase 1: A riddle will be given to each team related to a particular brand; they must solve it within 120 seconds.
  • Points: 1st place - 5 pts, 2nd place - 3 pts, Remaining teams - 1 pt.
  • Phase 2: Another riddle to find a product related to the brand; points same as Phase 1.
  • Teams design the product based on Phase 1 & 2.
    • 1st place to finish design: 10 pts
    • 2nd place: 7 pts
    • 3rd place: 5 pts
  • Maximum time for all teams: 2 hours.
  • Example: If the brand is BMW and the product is a pen, teams design the pen as if BMW manufactured it.
  • PCs will be provided by organizers.
  • Use of AI tools or image references leads to immediate disqualification.

Round 2 (KUSHALA)

  • Teams that designed the product in Round 1 must also create a marketing pamphlet for it.
  • Example: How would BMW market their pen if they manufactured it?
  • Points: 1st place - 10 pts, 2nd place - 7 pts, 3rd place - 5 pts.
  • The pamphlet design should be based on traditional vedic theme.
  • Time limit: 1.5 hours.

Pre-assignment Round (CHITRAKALA)

  • Any time before the event starts the organisers will give a pre assignment all the participants should do the pre assignment.
  • Note:-"IT CONSISTS MARKS".
