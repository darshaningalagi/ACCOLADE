Gaming Event - ACCOLADE #11.25

List of Games

  • Treasure Hunt
  • BGMI

Requirements for Gaming Competition

  • Bring your accessories like headphones, finger gloves, and charger.

General Rules and Regulations

  • Each team consists of 4 members.
  • No third-party applications or cheat codes will be allowed.
  • If any participant is found cheating, that team will be disqualified immediately.
  • Understand and follow the rules of the games as given by the organizers.
  • Participants must stay in the game until it officially ends.

Treasure Hunt Rules

  • The duration of the treasure hunt is 3 hours.
  • Teams must find the clues in the sequence provided by organizers.
  • Teams must stay together within sight and not run ahead.
  • No use of mobile phones while solving clues.
  • Cheating will result in immediate team disqualification.
  • The goal is to complete the task within the given time.

BGMI Rules

  • The use of aimbot, trigger bots, ESP, or any hacking tools is strictly prohibited.
  • Using any hacking application on your device will lead to disqualification.
  • Team up with opponents is prohibited.
  • Teams will be assigned slots according to numbers.
  • There will be three matches:
    • Erangel
    • Sanhok
    • Miramar

BGMI Point System

  • Winner Winner Chicken Dinner: 10 points
  • Placement points: 10 points
  • Finish points: 1 point per kill
