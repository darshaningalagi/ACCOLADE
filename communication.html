Communication Event Rules - ACCOLADE #11.25

Requirements

  • Two Participants from each team.
  • One Laptop compulsory in each Team.
  • Pre-assignment will be given.

Round 1: Presentation Breakdown

  • There will be two participants in each team; you must decide who will prepare the PPT and who will explain.
  • Participant 1 will prepare the PPT. Preparation time is 15 minutes.
  • PPT should contain at least 7 slides.
  • Participant 2 will have 10 minutes to prepare on the same PPT and then present.
  • Further details will be disclosed on the spot.
  • Judges decision will be final.

Round 2: Harmony in Conversation

  • There will be two participants in each team.
  • One participant will watch the video, the other will listen to the audio of the same video.
  • Afterward, both participants will sit together and write a story of 10 to 15 lines based on the video.
  • Some questions will then be displayed based on the video, which should be answered after writing the story.
  • Total time to write the story and answer questions is 20 minutes.
  • After this round, there will be elimination.
  • Further details will be disclosed on the spot.
  • Judges decision will be final.

Round 3: Knight of Round

  • This round will be a surprise round.
  • Both participants must participate.
  • Judges decision will be final.
